# Team Iran vs Team USA - Deep Game Analysis

## 🎮 **Game Overview**
Team Iran vs Team USA is a sophisticated **Telegram Mini Game** that combines **PvP combat**, **territorial control**, **military strategy**, and **monetization** in a geopolitical gaming experience.

---

## 🏛️ **Core Game Loop**

### **1. Faction Selection**
- **Iran 🇮🇷**: Defense-focused, +20% defense bonus, desert/mountain warfare advantages
- **USA 🇺🇸**: Attack-focused, +20% attack bonus, urban/industrial warfare advantages
- **Permanent Choice**: Faction selection is binding and affects all gameplay mechanics

### **2. Resource Economy**
- **STG Token**: Primary in-game currency for battles, purchases, and trading
- **Multiple Acquisition Methods**:
  - **Real Money**: Stars (Telegram) and TON (blockchain) purchases
  - **Gameplay**: Battle victories, territory control, daily rewards
  - **Social**: Tipping opponents, gift exchanges

### **3. Progressive Gameplay**
- **Level System**: XP from battles, unlocks weapons and features
- **Military Arsenal**: 18+ weapons across 5 categories (Tanks, Missiles, Drones, Warships, Aircraft)
- **Territory Control**: 6 strategic locations with dynamic bonuses

---

## ⚔️ **Battle System Deep Dive**

### **Turn-Based Combat Mechanics**
```
Battle Flow:
1. Weapon Selection → 2. Territory Selection → 3. Wager Setting → 4. Matchmaking → 5. Battle Execution
```

### **Combat Formula**
```
Base Damage = (Player Level × 10) + Random(0-20)
Final Damage = Max(1, (Base Damage - Defense) × Weapon_Bonus × Territory_Bonus × Faction_Bonus)
```

### **Strategic Elements**
- **30-second turn limits** for tactical decision-making
- **4 Action Types**: Attack, Defend (20% damage reduction), Special, Skip
- **Critical Hits**: Weapon-specific chance (2-22%) for 1.5x damage
- **Faction Special Abilities**:
  - **Iran - "Desert Storm"**: Reduces opponent accuracy by 30%
  - **USA - "Air Strike"**: Guaranteed critical hit

## 🎯 **Strategic Depth**

### **Faction Advantages**
- **Iran**: Better in desert/mountain territories, defensive playstyle
- **USA**: Better in urban/industrial territories, aggressive playstyle
- **Cross-Faction Battles**: 100% matchmaking probability (encouraged)
- **Same-Faction Battles**: 70% probability (training/sparring)

### **Weapon Synergy**
- **Faction-Specific Bonuses**: Iran weapons get 1.2-1.4x bonus in Iran territories
- **USA weapons get 1.3-1.4x bonus in USA territories**
- **Terrain Matching**: Desert weapons excel in desert territories, etc.

### **Economic Strategy**
- **Territory Investment**: Upgrade territories for better STG generation
- **Weapon Portfolio**: Balance between offense and defense
- **Risk Management**: Wager sizing based on opponent strength and territory bonuses
