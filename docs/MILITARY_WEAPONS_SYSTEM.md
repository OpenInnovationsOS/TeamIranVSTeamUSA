## Weapon Statistics Explained

### **Combat Stats**
- **Damage (DMG)**: Offensive power against enemy targets
- **Defense (DEF)**: Protective capabilities and resistance to attacks
- **Speed (SPD)**: Movement and response time in combat
- **Range (RNG)**: Effective engagement distance
- **Accuracy (ACC)**: Probability of hitting intended targets

### **Rarity Tiers**
- **Rare** (Blue): Standard military equipment
- **Epic** (Purple): Advanced systems with enhanced capabilities
- **Legendary** (Orange): Elite military hardware
- **Mythic** (Pink): Ultimate strategic assets

### **Faction Alignment**
- **USA Weapons**: Emphasize technology, precision, and air power
- **Iran Weapons**: Focus on defense, resilience, and asymmetric warfare

## Special Abilities System

### **Tank Abilities**
- **Armor Piercing Rounds**: Ignores 50% of enemy defense
- **Reactive Armor Defense**: 30% damage reduction for first 3 attacks
- **Urban Combat Mode**: +25% damage in city territories

### **Missile Abilities**
- **Ballistic Missile Interception**: Auto-defends against incoming missiles
- **Multi-Target Engagement**: Can attack 2-3 enemies simultaneously
- **Precision Strike**: 100% accuracy against stationary targets

### **Drone Abilities**
- **24-Hour Surveillance**: Reveals enemy positions on map
- **Loitering Attack Mode**: Can patrol and strike when enemies appear
- **Advanced Optics System**: +20% accuracy at night

### **Warship Abilities**
- **Carrier Air Wing Deployment**: Launches additional fighter attacks
- **AEGIS Defense System**: Auto-defends against all missile types
- **Stealth Mode Operation**: 50% chance to avoid detection

### **Aircraft Abilities**
- **Stealth Mode**: Cannot be targeted for first 2 turns
- **Supermaneuverability Combat**: +30% dodge chance
- **Air Dominance Mode**: +25% damage against other aircraft
- **Advanced Radar Tracking**: Never misses moving targets
- **Tank Hunter Mode**: +50% damage against ground vehicles
- **Night Attack Capability**: +40% accuracy in dark conditions

### **Level Requirements**
- **Level 6**: Basic drones (Shahed-136)

### **Investment Strategy**
- **Budget Players**: Focus on drones and basic tanks
